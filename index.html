Favorite Exercises

The Overhead Press is one of my favorite exercises to do when using a barbell/EzBar! It targets the pectorals, deltoids, triceps, and the trapezius.

The Bent Over Barbell Row targets the latissimus dorsi, spinal erectors, and scapular stabilizers.

The Upright Barbell Row is basically the same as the Bent Over Barbell Row, just standing up. Doing this exercise while standing targets the trapezius, deltoids, biceps, upper back, and the core.
